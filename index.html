MISSION

Dedicated to fostering a scientific, innovative, collaborative, and entrepreneurial culture at West Visayas State University (WVSU), we strive to meet the growing demand for advancing university research and development, facilitating the commercialization of emerging technologies, and enhancing their societal impact.

VISION

A thriving ecosystem at West Visayas State University (WVSU) that leads in scientific innovation and technology transfer, empowering communities through effective commercialization and sustainable development.

ABOUT US

OUR GOALS

To develop and nurture a more scientific, innovative, collaborative, and entrepreneurial culture of WVSU.

Intends to progressively address the increasing demand to advance university research and development, and other potential technologies developed for the benefit of society.

OUR OBJECTIVES

To harmonize the Innovation and Technology Support Office (ITSO), the BINHI Technology Business Incubator (TBI), and the Knowledge and Technology Transfer Office (KTTO).

To establish policies and processes engaging technology transfer and R&D commercialization initiatives.

To implement strategies and protocols to increase the number of commercially viable technologies.

To create and develop a team of experts in the field of intellectual property management and R&D commercialization.

To build a strong academe-industry partnership especially in consulting, research, technology transfer, and commercialization.

To establish and maintain an innovation fund for research and innovation.

OUR SERVICES

IP Management

IP Management involves protecting and managing intellectual property rights, ensuring legal compliance, and maximizing innovation potential.

Technology Transfer & Promotion

Technology Transfer & Promotion bridges the gap between research institutions and industry by facilitating commercialization of innovative technologies.

Industry Matching & Partnership

Industry Matching & Partnership connects businesses with research institutions to foster collaboration and innovation-driven partnerships.

R&D Commercialization

R&D Commercialization transforms research outcomes into viable products and services, benefiting industries and society.
